Magento 2 Request For Quote



The MilopleMagento 2 Call for Price extension allows you to hide the price of products on your product pages and display a "Call for Price" button instead. This can be useful for products that have variable pricing, such as custom-made products or products that are sold in bulk.

To use the Milople Magento 2 Call for Price extension, you will need to:

  1. Install the extension on your Magento 2 store.
  2. Configure the extension settings.
  3. Select the products that you want to hide the price for.
  4. Customize the "Call for Price" button.

Once the extension is configured, you can enable it for individual products, categories, or globally. You can also customize the text and color of the "Call for Price" button.

Here are some of the benefits of using the Milople Magento 2 Call for Price extension:

  • Increased customer engagement: When customers see a "Call for Price" button, they are more likely to contact you to learn more about the product and get a quote.
  • Improved sales conversions: By engaging with customers and providing them with personalized quotes, you can increase your sales conversions.
  • Reduced returns: By making sure that customers are aware of the price before they buy, you can reduce the number of returns.

Here are some tips for using the Milople Magento 2 Call for Price extension effectively:

  • Make sure to clearly explain why the price of the product is not displayed on the product page.
  • Include a contact form on the product page so that customers can easily request a quote.
  • Respond to customer inquiries promptly and professionally.
  • Offer discounts and promotions to customers who contact you for a quote.

By following these tips, you can use the Milople Magento 2 Call for Price extension to increase your sales and improve the customer experience.
