Magento 2 Magpie Payment Gateway



Milople Magento 2 Magpie Payment Gateway is a Magento 2 extension that allows you to accept payments from your customers using the Magpie payment gateway. Magpie is a popular payment gateway in India, and it offers a variety of payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, net banking, and mobile wallets.

The extension is easy to install and configure, and it offers a number of features that make it a good choice for Magento 2 stores, including:

  • Support for all major payment methods in India
  • Secure storage of customer payment information
  • Fraud prevention features
  • Easy integration with Magento 2

To install Milople Magento 2 Magpie Payment Gateway, you can follow these steps:

  1. Download the extension from the Milople website.
  2. Unzip the extension file and copy it to the app/code directory of your Magento 2 installation.
  3. Run the following commands to install the extension:
php bin/magento setup:upgrade
php bin/magento setup:di:compile
php bin/magento setup:static-content:deploy

Once the extension is installed, you can configure it by going to Stores > Configuration > Sales > Payment Methods and selecting Magpie.

Here, you will need to enter your Magpie merchant ID and merchant key, and choose the payment options that you want to offer to your customers. You can also choose to save customer payment information for future purchases and to enable fraud prevention features.

Once you have configured the extension, your customers will be able to pay for their purchases using Magpie at checkout.

Here are some of the benefits of using Milople Magento 2 Magpie Payment Gateway:

  • Accept payments from Indian customers: Magpie is a popular payment gateway in India, so it is a good choice for Magento 2 stores that want to accept payments from Indian customers.
  • Wide range of payment methods: Magpie offers a variety of payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, net banking, and mobile wallets. This gives your customers the flexibility to choose the payment method that they prefer.
  • Secure payments: Magpie is a PCI DSS Level 1 compliant payment gateway, which means that it uses the highest level of security to protect customer payment information.
  • Easy to use: The extension is easy to install and configure, and it offers a seamless checkout experience for your customers.

If you are looking for a payment gateway that allows you to accept payments from Indian customers in a variety of payment methods, then Milople Magento 2 Magpie Payment Gateway is a good option to consider.
