Magento 2 Product Attachments



Milople Magento 2 Product Attachments extension allows you to upload, manage, and display attachments on your product pages. This can be useful for providing customers with additional information about your products, such as user manuals, technical specifications, or warranty information.

The extension supports a variety of file formats, including PDFs, images, videos, and audio files. You can also choose to display the attachments on the product tab or in the product details section.

Here are some of the benefits of using Milople Magento 2 Product Attachments extension:

  • Improved customer experience: Providing customers with additional information about your products can help them make informed purchase decisions.
  • Increased sales: By providing customers with the information they need, you can increase sales and reduce the number of product returns.
  • Improved SEO: Attachments can be indexed by search engines, which can help to improve your website's SEO ranking.
  • Reduced customer support costs: By providing customers with the information they need upfront, you can reduce the number of customer support inquiries.

To use Milople Magento 2 Product Attachments extension, simply install the extension on your Magento 2 store and then configure it to your liking. Once you've uploaded and configured your attachments, they will be automatically displayed on your product pages.

Here are some specific examples of how you can use Milople Magento 2 Product Attachments extension:

  • Upload a user manual for a product.
  • Upload technical specifications for a product.
  • Upload a warranty information document for a product.
  • Upload assembly instructions for a product.
  • Upload a video demonstration of a product.
  • Upload customer testimonials for a product.

If you're looking for a way to improve the customer experience, increase sales, improve SEO, and reduce customer support costs, Milople Magento 2 Product Attachments extension is a great option to consider.
