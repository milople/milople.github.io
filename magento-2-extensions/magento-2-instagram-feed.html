Magento 2 Instagram Feed



The Milople Magento 2 Instagram Feed extension is a powerful and flexible extension that allows you to display your Instagram feed on your Magento 2 store.

The extension is easy to use and configure. Once you have installed the extension, you can simply go to the extension's configuration page and connect your Instagram account.

You can then select the Instagram feed that you want to display on your store and choose the layout and design options.

The Milople Magento 2 Instagram Feed extension allows you to display your Instagram feed in a variety of ways, including:

  • Grid
  • Slider
  • Carousel
  • Wall
  • Popup

You can also customize the appearance of your Instagram feed by choosing the following options:

  • Number of images to display
  • Image size
  • Image border
  • Image margin
  • Caption text
  • Caption font size
  • Caption font color
  • Call to action button text
  • Call to action button color
  • Call to action button URL
