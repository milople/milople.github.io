Magento 2 WhatsApp Share



The Milople Magento 2 WhatsApp Share extension allows you to easily share products and other related information from your Magento 2 store directly using the WhatsApp messaging app. This can be a great way to increase sales and engagement with your customers.

To use the Milople Magento 2 WhatsApp Share extension, you will need to:

  1. Install the extension on your Magento 2 store.
  2. Configure the extension settings.
  3. Enable the WhatsApp Share button on your product pages.

Once the extension is enabled, customers will be able to share your products and other related information with their WhatsApp contacts by clicking on the WhatsApp Share button.

The Milople Magento 2 WhatsApp Share extension provides a number of features that make it easy to share products and other related information on WhatsApp, including:

  • Customizable WhatsApp Share button: You can customize the appearance of the WhatsApp Share button to match your store's branding.
  • Customizable share message: You can customize the share message that is sent to WhatsApp users when they click on the WhatsApp Share button.
  • Support for product pages, category pages, and CMS pages: The WhatsApp Share button can be enabled on product pages, category pages, and CMS pages.
  • Support for multiple languages: The WhatsApp Share extension supports multiple languages, so you can use it in your store's native language.

Here are some of the benefits of using the Milople Magento 2 WhatsApp Share extension:

  • Increase sales: The WhatsApp Share extension can help you increase sales by making it easy for customers to share your products with their WhatsApp contacts.
  • Improve customer engagement: The WhatsApp Share extension can help you improve customer engagement by allowing customers to interact with your store on WhatsApp, which is a messaging app that they are already familiar with.
  • Increase brand awareness: The WhatsApp Share extension can help you increase brand awareness by exposing your products to a wider audience through WhatsApp.
  • Boost conversion rates: The WhatsApp Share extension can help you boost conversion rates by making it easier for customers to complete their purchases.

If you are looking for a way to increase sales, improve customer engagement, increase brand awareness, and boost conversion rates, then the Milople Magento 2 WhatsApp Share extension is a great option for you.
