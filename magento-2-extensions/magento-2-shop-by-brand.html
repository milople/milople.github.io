Magento 2 Shop By Brand



The Milople Magento 2 Shop by Brand extension is a powerful and flexible extension that allows you to create and manage a dedicated brand page for each brand that you sell in your store.

The extension is easy to use and configure. Once you have installed the extension, you can simply go to the extension's configuration page and create new brand pages.

For each brand page, you can specify the following:

  • Brand name: The name of the brand.
  • Brand logo: The logo of the brand.
  • Brand description: A brief description of the brand.
  • Brand products: The products that belong to the brand.
  • Brand page layout and design: You can customize the layout and design of each brand page using a variety of options, such as the brand page banner image, brand page header, brand page footer, and brand page sidebar.

You can also display your brand pages in a variety of ways, including:

  • Brand page menu: You can display a link to your brand pages in the main navigation menu of your store.
  • Brand page sidebar widget: You can display a brand page sidebar widget on the product category pages and product pages of your store.
  • Brand page search engine optimization: You can optimize your brand pages for search engines by adding custom meta titles, meta descriptions, and keywords.

The Milople Magento 2 Shop by Brand extension is a great way to improve the customer experience of your store. By providing customers with a dedicated page for each brand, you can make it easier for them to find and browse the products that they are interested in.

Here are some of the benefits of using the Milople Magento 2 Shop by Brand extension:

  • Improve customer experience: By providing customers with a dedicated page for each brand, you can make it easier for them to find and browse the products that they are interested in. This can lead to increased sales and improved customer satisfaction.
  • Increase brand awareness: By creating dedicated brand pages, you can increase brand awareness and promote your brand identity.
  • Boost SEO: You can optimize your brand pages for search engines by adding custom meta titles, meta descriptions, and keywords. This can help to improve your website's ranking in search engine results pages (SERPs).
  • Improve product organization: Brand pages can help you to organize your products in a logical way, making it easier for customers to find the products that they are looking for.
  • Create a more professional and engaging shopping experience: By providing customers with dedicated brand pages, you can create a more professional and engaging shopping experience. This can lead to increased sales and improved customer loyalty.

Overall, the Milople Magento 2 Shop by Brand extension is a valuable tool for any Magento 2 store owner who wants to improve the customer experience, increase brand awareness, boost SEO, improve product organization, and create a more professional and engaging shopping experience.

  • Create a dedicated brand page for each of the brands that you sell in your store.
  • Display your brand pages in the main navigation menu of your store.
