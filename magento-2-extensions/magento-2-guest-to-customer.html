Magento 2 Guest to Customer



The Milople Magento 2 Guest to Customer extension is a powerful and flexible extension that allows you to automatically convert guest users into registered customers.

The extension is easy to use and configure. Once you have installed the extension, you can simply go to the extension's configuration page and enable it. You can also specify the following options:

  • Automatically convert guest users after checkout: You can choose to automatically convert guest users into registered customers immediately after they checkout, or you can choose to send them an email with a link to confirm their registration.
  • Customer group assignment: You can assign new customers to a specific customer group.
  • Email notification: You can send an email notification to new customers to welcome them to your store and to provide them with information about their account.

The Milople Magento 2 Guest to Customer extension offers a number of benefits, including:

  • Increase customer registration rates: By automatically converting guest users into registered customers, you can increase your customer registration rates. This can lead to increased sales and improved customer loyalty.
  • Reduce cart abandonment: Cart abandonment is a major problem for e-commerce businesses. The Milople Magento 2 Guest to Customer extension can help to reduce cart abandonment by making it easier for customers to checkout as guests.
  • Improve customer relationship management (CRM): By having a complete database of registered customers, you can improve your CRM efforts. This can help you to target your marketing campaigns more effectively and to provide better customer service.
  • Simplify compliance: Some industries, such as the financial services industry, have regulations that require businesses to collect certain information from their customers. The Milople Magento 2 Guest to Customer extension can help you to comply with these regulations by automatically collecting customer information during the checkout process.

Overall, the Milople Magento 2 Guest to Customer extension is a valuable tool for any e-commerce business that wants to increase customer registration rates, reduce cart abandonment, improve CRM, and simplify compliance.
