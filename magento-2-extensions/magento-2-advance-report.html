Magento 2 Advanced Report



The Milople Magento 2 Advanced Report extension is a powerful tool that can help you gain valuable insights into your Magento 2 store performance. It provides a wide range of reports that cover all aspects of your store, including sales, orders, customers, products, and inventory.

To use the Milople Magento 2 Advanced Report extension, you will need to:

  1. Install the extension on your Magento 2 store.
  2. Configure the extension settings.
  3. Select the reports that you want to generate.
  4. Generate the reports.

The Milople Magento 2 Advanced Report extension provides a variety of different types of reports, including:

  • Sales reports: These reports provide detailed information about your sales, such as total sales, sales by product, sales by customer, and sales by channel.
  • Order reports: These reports provide detailed information about your orders, such as the number of orders, order value, order status, and shipping method.
  • Customer reports: These reports provide detailed information about your customers, such as the number of customers, customer spending habits, and customer demographics.
  • Product reports: These reports provide detailed information about your products, such as the number of products sold, product revenue, and product inventory levels.
  • Inventory reports: These reports provide detailed information about your inventory, such as stock levels, stock value, and low stock alerts.

You can also use the Milople Magento 2 Advanced Report extension to create custom reports. This allows you to generate reports that are tailored to your specific needs.

Here are some of the benefits of using the Milople Magento 2 Advanced Report extension:

  • Gain valuable insights into your store performance
  • Identify areas for improvement
  • Make better business decisions
  • Increase sales and profits

Here are some tips for using the Milople Magento 2 Advanced Report extension effectively:

  • Schedule reports to be generated automatically. This will save you time and ensure that you always have up-to-date reports.
  • Use the reports to track your progress over time. This will help you identify trends and patterns.
  • Compare your results to industry benchmarks. This will help you determine how your store is performing compared to other stores in your industry.
  • Use the reports to create targeted marketing campaigns. For example, you could use the reports to identify customers who have not purchased from you in a while and send them a special offer.

By following these tips, you can use the Milople Magento 2 Advanced Report extension to gain valuable insights into your store performance and make better business decisions.
