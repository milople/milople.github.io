Magento 2 Low Stock Notification Email to Admin



The Milople Magento 2 Low Stock Notification Email to Admin extension is a simple yet powerful extension that allows you to send automated low stock notifications to your Magento 2 store admins.

The extension is easy to configure and use. Once you have installed the extension, you can simply go to the extension's configuration page and set the following options:

  • Notify threshold: This is the stock level at which you want to start receiving low stock notifications.
  • Email recipient: This is the email address of the admin who should receive the low stock notifications.
  • Email subject: This is the subject line of the low stock notification email.
  • Email body: This is the body of the low stock notification email.

You can also customize the email body to include additional information, such as the product's SKU, image, category, price, and supplier.

Once you have configured the extension, it will start sending automatic low stock notifications to your admins whenever the stock level of a product falls below the notify threshold.

The Milople Magento 2 Low Stock Notification Email to Admin extension is a great way to ensure that your admins are always aware of low stock levels, so that they can take the necessary action to ensure that your products do not go out of stock.

Here are some of the benefits of using the Milople Magento 2 Low Stock Notification Email to Admin extension:

  • Reduce the risk of stockouts: By receiving automated low stock notifications, your admins can quickly take action to replenish stock and avoid stockouts.
  • Improve customer satisfaction: Stockouts can lead to disappointed customers, so avoiding them is important for customer satisfaction.
  • Increase sales: By avoiding stockouts, you can ensure that your customers are always able to purchase the products they want, which can lead to increased sales.
  • Reduce workload for admins: The Milople Magento 2 Low Stock Notification Email to Admin extension automates the process of sending low stock notifications, which can save your admins time and effort.

Overall, the Milople Magento 2 Low Stock Notification Email to Admin extension is a valuable tool for any Magento 2 store owner. It is easy to use and configure, and it can help you to reduce the risk of stockouts, improve customer satisfaction, increase sales, and reduce workload for admins.
