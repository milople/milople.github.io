Magento 2 Product Label



The Milople Magento 2 Product Label extension allows you to add custom labels to your product pages. This can be a great way to highlight new products, sale items, or other important information.

To use the Milople Magento 2 Product Label extension, you will need to:

  1. Install the extension on your Magento 2 store.
  2. Configure the extension settings.
  3. Create product labels.
  4. Assign product labels to products.
  5. Display product labels on product pages.

The Milople Magento 2 Product Label extension allows you to create a variety of different types of labels, including:

  • Text labels
  • Image labels
  • HTML labels

You can also customize the appearance of your labels by choosing the font, size, color, and alignment of the text.

Once you have created your product labels, you can assign them to products individually or in bulk. You can also choose to display product labels on specific product pages, categories, or globally.

Here are some of the benefits of using the Milople Magento 2 Product Label extension:

  • Highlight new products, sale items, and other important information
  • Improve customer engagement
  • Increase sales and conversions
  • Reduce returns

Here are some tips for using the Milople Magento 2 Product Label extension effectively:

  • Use product labels to highlight important information, such as new products, sale items, and free shipping.
  • Be strategic about where you place your product labels. Don't overload your product pages with too many labels.
  • Use clear and concise text on your product labels.
  • Make sure your product labels are visually appealing and match the overall design of your product pages.

By following these tips, you can use the Milople Magento 2 Product Label extension to improve your customer experience and increase sales.
