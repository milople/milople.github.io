Magento 2 Request For Quote



The Milople Magento 2 Request for Quote (RFQ) extension allows your customers to request a quote for products on your website. This can be useful for customers who are interested in purchasing products in bulk or who need a custom quote.

To use the Milople Magento 2 Request For Quote extension, you will need to:

  1. Install the extension on your Magento 2 store.
  2. Configure the extension settings.
  3. Select the products that you want to allow customers to request a quote for.
  4. Customize the RFQ form.

Once the extension is configured, customers will be able to request a quote for products by clicking on the "Request for Quote" button on the product page. They will then be able to fill out a form with their contact information and the quantity of products they are interested in.

You will receive an email notification when a customer requests a quote. You can then review the quote and send the customer a response.

Here are some of the benefits of using the Milople Magento 2 Request For Quote extension:

  • Increased sales: By making it easy for customers to request quotes, you can increase your sales.
  • Improved customer satisfaction: Customers appreciate being able to get a quote for products before they buy, especially for large or custom orders.
  • Reduced returns: By making sure that customers are aware of the price and specifications of products before they buy, you can reduce the number of returns.

Here are some tips for using the Milople Magento 2 RFQ extension effectively:

  • Make sure to clearly explain how the RFQ process works on your website.
  • Respond to customer requests promptly and professionally.
  • Offer discounts and promotions to customers who request a quote.

By following these tips, you can use the Milople Magento 2 RFQ extension to increase your sales and improve the customer experience.
