Magento 2 Pre Order Extension



The Milople Magento 2 Pre-Order Extension is a powerful and flexible extension that allows you to accept pre-orders for your products.

The extension is easy to use and configure. Once you have installed the extension, you can simply go to the extension's configuration page and enable pre-orders for the products that you want to pre-sell.

You can also specify the following options for each product:

  • Pre-order start date: The date on which pre-orders will open.
  • Pre-order end date: The date on which pre-orders will close.
  • Pre-order price: The price at which customers can pre-order the product.
  • Pre-order deposit: The amount of deposit that customers must pay to pre-order the product.
  • Pre-order payment methods: The payment methods that customers can use to pay for their pre-orders.
  • Pre-order notification email: The email template that will be sent to customers who pre-order the product.

When a customer pre-orders a product, they will be able to pay a deposit to secure their order. The full amount of the order will be charged to the customer's credit card on the pre-order release date.
