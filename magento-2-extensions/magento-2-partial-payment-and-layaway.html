Magento 2 Partial Payment and Layaway



The Milople Magento 2 Partial Payment and Layaway extension allows you to offer partial payment and layaway plans to your customers. This can be a great way to increase sales and make your products more affordable for customers with limited budgets.

To use the Milople Magento 2 Partial Payment and Layaway extension, you will need to:

  1. Install the extension on your Magento 2 store.
  2. Configure the extension settings.
  3. Set the down payment amount and the number of installments.
  4. Enable the extension for specific products or categories.
  5. Display the partial payment and layaway options on your product pages.

Once the extension is enabled, customers will be able to choose to pay for their purchases in installments. The down payment will be charged immediately, and the remaining installments will be charged at regular intervals.

The Milople Magento 2 Partial Payment and Layaway extension provides a number of features that make it easy to manage partial payment and layaway plans, including:

  • Automated payment reminders: The extension can automatically send payment reminders to customers who have missed a payment.
  • Late payment fees: You can charge late payment fees to customers who miss a payment.
  • Order cancellation: You can cancel orders if customers fail to make a payment.
