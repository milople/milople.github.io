Magento 2 Product Sort



Milople Magento 2 Product Sort is a Magento 2 extension that allows you to sort your products in a variety of ways, including by price, popularity, stock availability, and more. It also allows you to create custom sorting rules, so you can sort your products in the way that best meets your needs.

The extension is easy to install and configure, and it offers a number of features that make it a good choice for Magento 2 stores, including:

  • Support for a variety of sorting options
  • Ability to create custom sorting rules
  • Easy integration with Magento 2
  • Support for all Magento 2 versions

To install Milople Magento 2 Product Sort, you can follow these steps:

  1. Download the extension from the Milople website.
  2. Unzip the extension file and copy it to the app/code directory of your Magento 2 installation.
  3. Run the following commands to install the extension:
php bin/magento setup:upgrade
php bin/magento setup:di:compile
php bin/magento setup:static-content:deploy

Once the extension is installed, you can configure it by going to Stores > Configuration > Catalog > Product Sort.

Here, you can choose the default sorting option for your products, and you can also create custom sorting rules.

Once you have configured the extension, your products will be sorted in the way that you have specified.

Here are some of the benefits of using Milople Magento 2 Product Sort:

  • Improved customer experience: Customers can easily find the products that they are looking for by sorting the products in a way that is convenient for them.
  • Increased sales: By sorting your products in the right way, you can increase the visibility of your best-selling products and encourage customers to buy more products.
  • Reduced bounce rate: Customers are less likely to leave your store if they can easily find the products that they are looking for.
  • Improved SEO: By sorting your products in a way that is relevant to your target keywords, you can improve the SEO of your product pages.
