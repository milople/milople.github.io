Magento 2 Delivery Date and Time Extension



Milople Magento 2 Delivery Date and Time is a powerful extension that allows you to give customers the flexibility to choose their preferred delivery date and time. This can help you improve customer satisfaction and reduce the number of delivery-related issues.

With this extension, you can:

  • Allow customers to choose their preferred delivery date and time during the checkout process.
  • Set customizable time slots based on your operating capacity and customer needs.
  • Block out specific dates and times when deliveries are not available.
  • Specify the order preparation time for each product.
  • Send email notifications to customers confirming their delivery date and time.

The extension is also compatible with other popular Magento 2 extensions, such as Milople Magento 2 Order Editor and Milople Magento 2 Better Tier Price. This makes it easy to integrate the extension into your existing store workflow.

Here are some of the benefits of using Milople Magento 2 Delivery Date and Time:

  • Improved customer satisfaction: Customers appreciate the flexibility to choose their preferred delivery date and time. This can lead to increased customer satisfaction and loyalty.
  • Reduced delivery-related issues: By giving customers control over their delivery date and time, you can reduce the number of missed deliveries and other delivery-related issues.
  • Increased efficiency: The extension can help you streamline your delivery process by making it easier to schedule and track deliveries.
  • Improved transparency: The extension provides clear and concise information to customers about their delivery date and time. This can help to reduce customer inquiries and improve the customer experience.

Overall, Milople Magento 2 Delivery Date and Time is a valuable extension for any Magento 2 store that wants to improve customer satisfaction, reduce delivery-related issues, and increase efficiency.
