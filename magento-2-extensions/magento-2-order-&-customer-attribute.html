Magento 2 BetterTier Price



Magento 2 Better Tier Price extension by Milople is a powerful tool that allows you to create and manage tiered product pricing on your Magento 2 store. With this extension, you can:

  • Set different prices for products based on the quantity purchased.
  • Offer discounts for bulk purchases.
  • Create different tier price rules for different customer groups.
  • Display tier prices on the product page, in the shopping cart, and at checkout.
  • Easily manage all of your tier price rules from a single interface.

Here are some of the benefits of using Milople Magento 2 Better Tier Price:

  • Increase sales: Tiered pricing can encourage customers to buy more products by offering them discounts for bulk purchases.
  • Improve customer satisfaction: Tiered pricing can make your products more affordable for customers, which can lead to increased customer satisfaction.
  • Gain a competitive advantage: Tiered pricing can help you compete with other online retailers by offering your customers better prices.
  • Improve your profit margins: By offering discounts for bulk purchases, you can increase your profit margins on these sales.
