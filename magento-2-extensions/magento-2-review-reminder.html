Magento 2 Review Reminder



The Milople Magento 2 Review Reminder extension allows you to send automated email reminders to customers who have purchased products from your store but have not yet left a review. This can be a great way to increase the number of reviews on your product pages and improve your overall customer satisfaction.

To use the Milople Magento 2 Review Reminder extension, you will need to:

  1. Install the extension on your Magento 2 store.
  2. Configure the extension settings.
  3. Select the products that you want to send review reminders for.
  4. Set the schedule for sending review reminders.
  5. Customize the review reminder email.

Once the extension is configured, it will automatically send review reminder emails to customers who have purchased products from your store but have not yet left a review. The email will include a link to the product page where the customer can leave a review.

Here are some of the benefits of using the Milople Magento 2 Review Reminder extension:

  • Increased number of reviews: Review reminders can help you increase the number of reviews on your product pages, which can lead to increased sales and conversions.
  • Improved customer satisfaction: Customers appreciate being reminded to leave reviews, and they are more likely to be satisfied with their shopping experience if they are able to leave feedback.
  • Increased trust and credibility: Positive reviews can help you build trust and credibility with potential customers.

Here are some tips for using the Milople Magento 2 Review Reminder extension effectively:

  • Only send review reminders to customers who have purchased products from your store.
  • Don't send too many review reminders, or you may annoy your customers.
  • Personalize the review reminder email by including the customer's name and the product that they purchased.
  • Make it easy for customers to leave a review by including a link to the product page in the email.
  • Offer an incentive for leaving a review, such as a discount on future purchases.

By following these tips, you can use the Milople Magento 2 Review Reminder extension to increase the number of reviews on your product pages and improve your overall customer satisfaction.
