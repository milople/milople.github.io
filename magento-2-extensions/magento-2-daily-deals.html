Magento 2 Daily Deals



MilopleMagento 2 Daily Deals is a powerful extension that allows you to create and manage daily deals on your Magento 2 store. With this extension, you can:

  • Choose from a variety of deal types: percentage discounts, fixed amount reductions, or even "Buy One Get One" offers.
  • Schedule deals: Plan your deals ahead of time for specific days, weekends, or even weeks.
  • Add countdown timers: Create urgency and excitement by adding a countdown timer to each deal.
  • Showcase deals on the homepage and other pages: Promote your daily deals to customers throughout your store.
  • Track performance: Analyze the performance of your daily deals to see what's working and what's not.

Here are some of the benefits of using Milople Magento 2 Daily Deals:

  • Increase sales: Daily deals are a great way to boost sales and attract new customers.
  • Clear excess inventory: If you have excess inventory, you can use daily deals to sell it off quickly and efficiently.
  • Improve customer engagement: Daily deals give customers a reason to keep coming back to your store.
  • Grow your customer base: Daily deals can help you attract new customers and build a loyal customer base.

To use Milople Magento 2 Daily Deals, simply install the extension on your Magento 2 store and then configure it to your liking. Once you've created and scheduled your daily deals, they will be automatically displayed on your store's homepage and other pages. You can also track the performance of your daily deals to see what's working and what's not.

Overall, Milople Magento 2 Daily Deals is a powerful and easy-to-use extension that can help you boost sales, clear excess inventory, improve customer engagement, and grow your customer base.
