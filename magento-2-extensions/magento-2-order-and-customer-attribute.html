Magento 2 Order & Customer Attribute



The Milople Magento 2 Order & Customer Attribute extension allows you to collect additional information from your customers during checkout and from orders. This information can be used to improve your customer service, marketing campaigns, and product development.

To use the Milople Magento 2 Order & Customer Attribute extension, you will need to:

  1. Install the extension on your Magento 2 store.
  2. Configure the extension settings.
  3. Create custom attributes for customers and orders.
  4. Display the custom attributes on the checkout page and in the order management grid.

Once the extension is configured, you can start collecting additional information from your customers and orders. The information you collect can be used for a variety of purposes, such as:

  • Customer service: You can use the information to improve your customer service by providing customers with more personalized support. For example, if you know that a customer has a medical condition, you can make sure that they are aware of any relevant product information or policies.
  • Marketing campaigns: You can use the information to create more targeted marketing campaigns. For example, if you know that a customer is interested in a particular product category, you can send them targeted emails or promotions.
  • Product development: You can use the information to develop new products or improve existing products. For example, if you know that a lot of customers are requesting a particular feature, you can add that feature to your products.

The Milople Magento 2 Order & Customer Attribute extension is a powerful tool that can help you improve your business in a variety of ways. By collecting additional information from your customers and orders, you can improve your customer service, marketing campaigns, and product development.

Here are some tips for using the Milople Magento 2 Order & Customer Attribute extension effectively:

  • Only collect information that is relevant to your business and that you will use.
  • Make sure to get your customers' consent before collecting any personal information.
  • Use the information to improve your customer service, marketing campaigns, and product development.
  • Keep the information secure and confidential.

By following these tips, you can use the Milople Magento 2 Order & Customer Attribute extension to collect valuable information from your customers and orders and use that information to improve your business.
