Magento 2 Custom Price



The Milople Magento 2 Custom Price extension allows you to set custom prices for products on your Magento 2 store. This can be useful for a variety of reasons, such as offering discounts to certain customers, running special promotions, or selling products at a different price in different regions.

To use the Milople Magento 2 Custom Price extension, you will need to:

  1. Install the extension on your Magento 2 store.
  2. Configure the extension settings.
  3. Create custom price rules.
  4. Assign custom price rules to products.

Once the extension is enabled, you will be able to create custom price rules for specific products, customer groups, or time periods. You can also set different prices for products in different regions.

The Milople Magento 2 Custom Price extension provides a number of features that make it easy to manage custom prices, including:

  • Flexible pricing options: You can set custom prices for products, customer groups, time periods, and regions.
  • Bulk pricing: You can create custom price rules for multiple products at once.
  • Automated pricing: The extension can automatically apply custom prices to products based on the rules that you have created.

The Milople Magento 2 Custom Price extension is a great way to manage custom prices on your Magento 2 store. It is easy to use and provides a number of features that make it easy to create and manage custom price rules.

Here are some of the benefits of using the Milople Magento 2 Custom Price extension:

  • Increase sales: Custom pricing can help you increase sales by offering discounts to certain customers, running special promotions, or selling products at a different price in different regions.
  • Improve customer satisfaction: Customers appreciate the flexibility of being able to purchase products at a price that they are comfortable with.
  • Reduce churn: Custom pricing can help you reduce churn by making it more affordable for customers to stay subscribed to your products.
  • Boost conversion rates: Custom pricing can help you boost conversion rates by making it more likely that customers will complete their purchases.

If you are looking for a way to increase sales, improve customer satisfaction, reduce churn, and boost conversion rates, then the Milople Magento 2 Custom Price extension is a great option for you.
