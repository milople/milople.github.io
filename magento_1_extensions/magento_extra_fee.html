Understanding Deposit Payments

Extra fees, often referred to as additional charges, provide a customer-friendly approach to tailoring the payment process. Instead of a fixed price, customers can choose to add extra fees as needed, providing several benefits:

  • Convenience: Customers have the flexibility to add fees for specific services, rush deliveries, or any other customizable options.
