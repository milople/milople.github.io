Understanding Request for Quote (RFQ)

Request for Quote is a valuable tool that allows customers to negotiate the terms of a purchase. Here are the primary advantages of offering RFQ functionality:

  • Negotiation: Customers can negotiate prices, quantities, or other terms, fostering a sense of control and empowerment in the purchasing process.
  • B2B Engagement: RFQ functionality is especially valuable for business-to-business (B2B) transactions, where price flexibility is often a key factor.
  • Customization: RFQ allows for personalized requests, accommodating specific needs or variations in products.

Key Features of the Magento Request for Quote (RFQ) Extension

Our extension is meticulously designed to seamlessly integrate Request for Quote functionality into your Magento store. Here are the key features that set it apart:

  1. Request Submission: Enable customers to submit RFQs directly from the product pages, making the process user-friendly and convenient.
  2. Quote Management: Streamline the management of incoming RFQs, allowing for easy review and response.
  3. Negotiation Tools: Offer negotiation tools, allowing customers to propose counteroffers, creating a dynamic and interactive purchasing experience.
  4. Customized Pricing: Implement customized pricing options based on the RFQ, accommodating the negotiation process and customer-specific needs.
  5. Quote History: Maintain a history of RFQs and their status, providing transparency and a reference point for customers and sellers.
