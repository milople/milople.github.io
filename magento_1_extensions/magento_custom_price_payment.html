Understanding Custom Pricing

Custom pricing, also known as name-your-price or pay-what-you-want pricing, is a customer-centric approach that allows customers to set their own prices for products or services. This approach offers several benefits:

  • Customer Empowerment: Custom pricing empowers customers by allowing them to determine the value of a product or service based on their preferences and budget.
  • Increased Sales: By offering flexibility in pricing, businesses can attract a wider customer base and increase sales, especially for unique or personalized products.
  • Adaptability: Custom pricing is adaptable for various business scenarios, including promotions, donations, and catering to diverse customer needs.
